## What is DMC Weapons Reborn?

DMC Weapons Reborn is a NeoForge mod for Minecraft 1.21.1 that brings iconic weapons from the **Devil May Cry** series into Minecraft. Each weapon is faithfully recreated with custom 3D models, authentic sound effects, and unique combat behaviours.

## Features

- **Custom 3D models** — every weapon has a fully modelled in-world and inventory representation
- **Authentic sound effects** — weapon sounds sourced and recreated from the DMC series (swing, shot, and hit SFX per weapon)
- **Projectile entities** — guns fire real projectiles with collision and impact logic; Kalina Ann rockets produce explosion effects on block impact
- **Dedicated creative tab** — all items grouped under the Devil May Cry inventory tab

---

## Installation

1. Download and install **[NeoForge 21.1.65+](https://neoforged.net)** for Minecraft 1.21.1
2. Place the `DMC-Weapons-Reborn` `.jar` into your `/mods/` folder
3. Launch Minecraft with the NeoForge profile
